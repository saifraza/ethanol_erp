# Distillery ERP — Design System (Copper & Cask Heritage)

> Based on Stitch "Copper & Cask Heritage" theme.
> Light mode, warm copper accents, serif headings, clean professional layout.

## Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif)
- Body: Inter (sans-serif)

## Key Principles
1. Copper as single dominant accent
2. Flat cards with borders, no shadows
3. Serif headings for premium feel
4. Warm neutrals, not cold grays
5. Uppercase tracking labels for sections
